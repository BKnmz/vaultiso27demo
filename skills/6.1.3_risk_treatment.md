TASK:
Draft the "Risk Treatment Plan" for {{ org.name }}.
For each risk identified in the upstream risk assessment, define the treatment option and
map it to the relevant ISO 27001:2022 Annex A controls.

Treatment options are: Modify (apply control) | Avoid | Share (transfer) | Retain (accept)

The document must include:
1. Risk treatment selection criteria (when to modify vs. accept vs. transfer)
2. A risk treatment table mapping risks to Annex A controls
3. The residual risk after treatment and whether it is within risk appetite
4. Statement of Applicability reference (which Annex A controls are applied/excluded)
5. Risk owner sign-off requirements

Use the risks from the upstream risk assessment context.
If no upstream risks are available, create 5 representative risks for this organization.
Length: 500-700 words including the treatment table.

OUTPUT FORMAT: Markdown with the following sections:
1. Purpose
2. Treatment Selection Criteria
3. Risk Treatment Table (Risk | Treatment Option | Annex A Control(s) | Residual Risk | Owner)
4. Residual Risk Acceptance
5. Statement of Applicability Summary
6. Review Schedule
